Who We Are

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a licensed Section 8 non-profit organization operating seamlessly out of Chhattisgarh, India.

We work tirelessly aligning our core workflows with global sustainability metrics and national strategic goals, maintaining direct transparent standards across all operational frameworks.

Key Projects & Initiatives

#ProjectSewa

Project Sewa

Serving humanity with raw compassion by delivering nutritious food materials, distribution drives, and robust seasonal clothing to underprivileged zones.

Project BachpanSala
